At Xtreme Padel Zambia, our aim is to inspire a love for tennis while fostering a vibrant sports community in Zambia. We are committed to:

Encouraging individuals of all ages and skill levels to embrace physical activity through the exciting sport of tennis..

  • Offering top-notch tennis courts and amenities that rival international standards, ensuring players enjoy an unparalleled experience.
  • Catering to diverse needs with dedicated courts for men and women, group packages, and accessible booking options for everyone..
  • Empowering players to reach their full potential by offering high-quality training sessions and resources.

Our ultimate goal is to make tennis accessible, enjoyable, and rewarding for everyone, and to establish Xtreme Padel Zambia as the go-to destination for tennis enthusiasts across the nation.

At Xtreme Padel Zambia, we take pride in delivering an exceptional tennis experience through our top-tier facilities. Here’s what sets us apart:

Our world-class facilities are built with your satisfaction in mind, so every visit to Xtreme Padel Zambia is nothing short of exceptional!

  • Our courts are designed to meet global benchmarks, ensuring the perfect surface for every game, from casual play to competitive tournaments.
  • We offer separate courts for men and women, providing comfort, privacy, and a focused environment for all players.
  • Our courts are meticulously maintained to ensure optimal performance, safety, and aesthetics, making every match enjoyable.
  • Enjoy access to comfortable seating, shaded areas, and clean, well-equipped changing rooms that add to your overall experience.

At Xtreme Padel Zambia, we believe tennis should be accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Our commitment to inclusivity is reflected in the following:

  • Whether you’re a casual player or a regular visitor, our user-friendly booking system allows you to reserve courts at times that suit your schedule.
  • Affordable Pricing and Packages: We offer competitive rates and customizable packages for individuals, groups, and events, making it easier for everyone to enjoy the game without breaking the bank.
  • Programs for All Skill Levels: From beginners to seasoned players, we welcome individuals of all abilities. Our training sessions and court facilities are designed to cater to players at every stage of their tennis journey.

By fostering inclusivity, Xtreme Padel Zambia ensures that everyone has the opportunity to experience the joy and benefits of tennis.

At Xtreme Padel Zambia, we are passionate about helping players unlock their full potential. Here’s how we support skill development for all levels.

With a focus on skill development, Xtreme Padel Zambia provides the tools, guidance, and opportunities needed to take your game to the next level.

  • Professional Coaching: Learn from experienced coaches who provide personalized training sessions tailored to your skill level, whether you’re a beginner mastering the basics or an advanced player refining your techniques.
  • Structured Training Programs: Our comprehensive training programs are designed to build your skills step by step, focusing on everything from footwork and strategy to improving your strokes and match play.
  • Practice Sessions and Drills: Access dedicated practice sessions that include specialized drills to improve your agility, endurance, and precision, ensuring continuous progress in your game.

Services

What we do offer

World-Class Tennis Court Rentals

Play on meticulously maintained courts built to meet international standards. Whether you're looking for a quick game or a full-day session, our courts are available for flexible booking durations, ensuring you get the best value for your time and money.

Dedicated Courts for Men and Women

Enjoy privacy and comfort with our gender-specific courts. Whether you're training or competing, our exclusive spaces are designed to create a secure and distraction-free environment for everyone.

Group and Tournament Packages

Hosting a friendly match with friends or planning a professional tournament? We offer tailored packages for groups and events, complete with on-site support and optional refreshments to make your event a success.

Coaching and Training Sessions

Sharpen your skills with access to expert coaching. Whether you're a beginner learning the basics or an advanced player perfecting your game, we provide structured training sessions suited to all skill levels.

Sports Equipment Rentals

Forget your racket or looking to try premium gear? We offer high-quality tennis equipment for rent, including rackets, balls, and other accessories, so you’re always game-ready.

Relaxation and Refreshment Areas

Take a break between matches at our comfortable lounge areas. Enjoy refreshments and light snacks to recharge while you prepare for your next game.
